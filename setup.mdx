After installing Musika, follow this guide to set up the app and customize it to your preferences.

## First Launch

When you launch Musika for the first time, you'll be greeted with the main interface. Here's how to get started:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Grant Permissions">
    Musika may request certain permissions to function properly:
    
    - **Storage Access**: Required for downloading songs and caching
    - **Media Access**: To access and play your music library
    - **Network Access**: For streaming music from YouTube Music
    - **Notification Access**: For playback controls and notifications
    
    <Info>
      Grant the permissions as needed. You can always modify these later in your device's app settings.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Explore the Interface">
    Familiarize yourself with the main sections of the app:
    
    - **Home**: Your personalized dashboard with recommendations
    - **Charts**: Browse trending and popular music
    - **Podcasts**: Discover audio podcast content
    - **Moods & Genres**: Explore music by mood and genre
    - **Library**: Access your playlists and downloaded content
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure Basic Settings">
    Navigate to the settings menu to customize your experience:
    
    1. Tap the menu icon or navigate to **Settings**
    2. Review and adjust key settings:
       - Audio quality preferences
       - Download quality
       - Theme preferences (Dark mode)
       - Playback settings
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Optional Configuration

### YouTube Music Account (Optional)

While Musika works without an account, you can optionally sign in to access your YouTube Music library:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Account Settings">
    Navigate to Settings > Account Management
  </Step>

  <Step title="Sign In">
    Follow the prompts to sign in with your Google account
    
    <Note>
      Signing in allows you to sync your YouTube Music playlists, liked songs, and listening history.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Sync Your Library">
    Once signed in, your playlists and liked songs will automatically sync
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Audio Quality Settings

Optimize your streaming and download quality based on your preferences:

1. Go to **Settings** > **Audio Quality**
2. Configure the following:
   - **Streaming Quality**: Choose between Low, Medium, High, or Auto (adapts to your connection)
   - **Download Quality**: Select quality for offline downloads
   - **Data Saver Mode**: Enable to reduce data usage on cellular connections

<Info>
  Higher quality audio provides better sound but uses more data and storage. Use Auto mode to let Musika adjust quality based on your network connection.
</Info>

### Playback Preferences

Customize how music plays:

1. Navigate to **Settings** > **Playback**
2. Adjust settings such as:
   - **Crossfade Duration**: Smooth transitions between tracks (Beta)
   - **Gapless Playback**: Enable seamless track transitions
   - **Playback Speed**: Adjust playback speed (0.5x - 2.0x)
   - **Audio Effects**: Configure equalizer and audio enhancements

### Download Management

Set up offline listening:

1. Go to **Settings** > **Downloads**
2. Configure:
   - **Download Location**: Choose where to store downloaded music
   - **Auto-Download**: Automatically download liked songs or playlists
   - **Download on WiFi Only**: Prevent downloads on cellular data

<Warning>
  Monitor your storage space when downloading music for offline listening. You can manage downloaded content from the Downloads section.
</Warning>

### Sleep Timer

Set up automatic playback control:

1. During playback, access the **Sleep Timer** from the player controls
2. Choose from preset durations or set a custom time
3. Enable **Fade Out** for gradual volume reduction before stopping

### Chromecast & DLNA Setup

Stream music to other devices:

**Chromecast**:
- Ensure your device and Chromecast are on the same WiFi network
- Tap the cast icon in the player to connect

**DLNA/UPnP**:
- Musika automatically discovers DLNA/UPnP devices on your local network
- Access compatible devices including Smart TVs, speakers, and media renderers
- Select your device from the streaming menu

<Info>
  DLNA/UPnP streaming is compatible with popular devices like Sonos speakers, Smart TVs, and other network media renderers.
</Info>

## Troubleshooting

If you encounter any issues during setup:

- **Playback Issues**: Check your internet connection and audio quality settings
- **Download Failures**: Ensure sufficient storage space and stable internet connection
- **Permission Errors**: Review app permissions in your device settings
- **Sync Problems**: Try signing out and back in to your YouTube Music account

<Note>
  For additional help and community support, visit the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/jed1boy/Musika) to report issues or ask questions.
</Note>
