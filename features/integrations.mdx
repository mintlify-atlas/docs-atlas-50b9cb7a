Musika seamlessly integrates with various platforms and services to provide a connected music experience across all your devices.

## Android Auto

Enjoy Musika on your car's display with full Android Auto support for safe, hands-free music control while driving.

### Features

- **Full Media Controls**: Play, pause, skip, and browse from your car display
- **Voice Commands**: Use Google Assistant for hands-free control
- **Now Playing Display**: See current track information on your dashboard
- **Browse Library**: Access your playlists, albums, and artists
- **Search**: Find music using voice or on-screen keyboard
- **Queue Management**: View and manage your playback queue

### Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your phone">
    Connect your Android device to your car via USB or wireless Android Auto
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Musika">
    On your car's display, tap the Android Auto icon and select Musika from the media apps
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Start playing">
    Browse your library or use voice commands to start playing music
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Voice Commands

Use Google Assistant with Android Auto:

- "Play music on Musika"
- "Play [artist name] on Musika"
- "Play [song name] on Musika"
- "Next song"
- "Pause music"
- "Resume music"

<Note>
  Android Auto requires Android 8.0 or higher and the Android Auto app on your phone.
</Note>

## Android TV

Musika supports Android TV with a leanback interface optimized for large screens and remote control navigation.

### Features

- **TV-Optimized Interface**: Easy navigation with remote control
- **Leanback Support**: Designed for the TV experience
- **Home Screen Banner**: Custom TV launcher banner
- **No Touchscreen Required**: Fully navigable with D-pad
- **Large Display**: Enjoy album art and lyrics on the big screen

### Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install on Android TV">
    Sideload the Musika APK to your Android TV device
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Launch from TV home">
    Find Musika in your apps list or on the leanback launcher
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Navigate with remote">
    Use your TV remote's D-pad to navigate the interface
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  For best results on Android TV, use a wireless keyboard or mouse for easier text input when searching.
</Tip>

## Last.fm Scrobbling

Connect your Last.fm account to automatically track your listening history and discover music based on your taste.

### What is Scrobbling?

Scrobbling automatically logs every song you listen to in Musika to your Last.fm profile, helping you:

- Track your listening habits over time
- Discover similar artists and recommendations
- Share your music taste with friends
- Generate personalized statistics

### Features

- **Automatic Scrobbling**: Songs are logged automatically as you listen
- **Now Playing Updates**: Real-time updates to your Last.fm profile
- **Configurable Thresholds**: Set when songs should be scrobbled
  - By percentage of song played
  - By minutes listened
  - Minimum track duration for scrobbling
- **Scrobble Delay**: Customize when scrobbles are submitted
- **Session Management**: Secure Last.fm authentication

### Setup Last.fm Integration

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Go to **Settings** → **Integrations** → **Last.fm**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Login to Last.fm">
    Tap **Login** and enter your Last.fm credentials
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Authorize Musika">
    Approve Musika to access your Last.fm account
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure scrobbling">
    Adjust scrobble settings:
    - Set scrobble percentage threshold (default: 50%)
    - Set minimum duration for scrobbling
    - Enable/disable Now Playing updates
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Scrobbling Settings

**Scrobble Threshold**  
Set when a song should be scrobbled:
- **By Percentage**: Scrobble after listening to X% of the song (default: 50%)
- **By Time**: Scrobble after X minutes of playback

**Minimum Duration**  
Only scrobble songs longer than a specified duration (prevents short tracks/interludes from being logged)

**Now Playing**  
Update your Last.fm "Now Playing" status in real-time as songs change

**Scrobble Delay**  
Wait before submitting scrobbles (useful if you skip songs frequently)

<Info>
  Last.fm scrobbling requires an active Last.fm account. Create one free at [last.fm](https://www.last.fm).
</Info>

### Troubleshooting Last.fm

<Accordion title="Songs not scrobbling">
  If your songs aren't appearing on Last.fm:
  
  1. Verify you're logged in to Last.fm in Musika
  2. Check internet connection (scrobbles require network)
  3. Ensure songs meet minimum duration threshold
  4. Check scrobble percentage setting isn't too high
  5. Review Last.fm service status
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Duplicate scrobbles">
  If songs are scrobbling multiple times:
  
  1. Disable scrobbling in other music apps
  2. Check scrobble delay settings
  3. Ensure you're not running multiple instances of Musika
</Accordion>

## Bluetooth Devices

Musika provides enhanced support for Bluetooth audio devices:

### Features

- **AVRCP Support**: Control playback from Bluetooth device buttons
- **Metadata Display**: Song info shown on compatible car stereos and headphones
- **Auto-Connect**: Automatically resume playback when connecting to trusted devices
- **Multiple Devices**: Switch between different Bluetooth devices seamlessly

### Supported Bluetooth Profiles

- **A2DP**: High-quality audio streaming
- **AVRCP**: Playback control (play, pause, skip, volume)
- **Bluetooth Low Energy**: For fitness trackers and wearables

<Note>
  Bluetooth permissions are optional. Grant them for the best experience with wireless devices.
</Note>
