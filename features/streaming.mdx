## Playback Modes

### Audio & Video Switching

Seamlessly switch between audio-only and video modes:

- **Audio-Only Mode**: Optimized for music listening with minimal data usage
- **Video Mode**: Watch music videos, live performances, and visual content
- **Instant Switching**: Toggle between modes without interrupting playback

<Tip>
  Use audio-only mode to save data and battery when you don't need the video component.
</Tip>

### Background Playback

Enjoy uninterrupted music while multitasking:

- **Background Service**: Music continues playing when you switch apps
- **Screen-Off Playback**: Listen with the screen off to save battery
- **Lock Screen Controls**: Full playback controls accessible from lock screen
- **Rich Notifications**: Media notifications with album artwork and controls

## Playback Controls

### Basic Controls

<Accordion title="Standard Playback">
  - **Play/Pause**: Tap to play or pause current track
  - **Skip Forward/Backward**: Navigate through your queue
  - **Seek Bar**: Jump to any position in the track by dragging
  - **Volume Control**: Adjust playback volume with smooth transitions
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Queue Management">
  - **Shuffle Mode**: Randomize playback order for variety
  - **Repeat Modes**: 
    - Repeat All: Loop through entire playlist/album
    - Repeat One: Continuously repeat current track
    - No Repeat: Play through queue once
</Accordion>

### Advanced Playback Features

#### Crossfade (Beta)

Smooth transitions between tracks for seamless listening:

- Gradually fade out the ending track while fading in the next
- Eliminates abrupt changes between songs
- Configurable fade duration
- Perfect for party playlists and continuous listening

<Note>
  Crossfade is currently in beta. You can enable it in Settings > Playback.
</Note>

#### Gapless Playback

Eliminates silence between tracks:

- Essential for live albums and continuous mixes
- Preserves the artist's intended listening experience
- Automatically detects and removes inter-track silence

#### Audio Effects

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Equalizer" icon="sliders">
    Built-in equalizer with presets and custom settings
  </Card>
  <Card title="Audio Enhancements" icon="sparkles">
    Bass boost, virtualization, and other sound effects

#### Playback Speed & Pitch

- **Variable Speed**: Adjust playback from 0.5x to 2.0x
  - 0.5x - 0.9x: Slow down to learn difficult passages
  - 1.1x - 2.0x: Speed up for faster listening
- **Independent Pitch Control**: Change pitch without affecting tempo
- **Use Cases**: Learning instruments, transcription, or creative listening

## Streaming Quality

### Adaptive Quality

Musika automatically adjusts streaming quality based on your connection:

- **WiFi**: High-quality audio for the best listening experience
- **Cellular**: Balanced quality to manage data usage
- **Poor Connection**: Lower quality to prevent buffering

### Data Saver Mode

<Tip>
  Enable Data Saver mode in Settings to reduce data consumption on cellular networks. This lowers streaming quality while maintaining acceptable audio fidelity.
</Tip>

## Troubleshooting

<Accordion title="Playback Issues">
  **Buffering or Stuttering**:
  - Check your internet connection speed
  - Enable Data Saver mode for poor connections
  - Clear app cache in Settings > Storage
  - Try lowering the streaming quality

  **No Sound**:
  - Verify media volume is not muted
  - Check if other apps can play audio
  - Disconnect and reconnect Bluetooth devices
  - Restart the app
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Connection Issues">
  **Can't Load Content**:
  - Verify internet connectivity
  - Sign in to your YouTube account if required
  - Check if YouTube Music is accessible in your region
  - Clear app data and sign in again

  **Frequent Disconnections**:
  - Switch to a more stable network
  - Disable battery optimization for Musika
  - Keep the app updated to the latest version
</Accordion>
