Musika includes powerful audio recognition capabilities that let you identify songs playing in your environment, discover new music, and instantly add identified tracks to your library.

## Overview

### What is Audio Recognition?

Audio recognition (also known as music identification or audio fingerprinting) analyzes the audio in your environment and identifies the song that's playing. Musika uses advanced audio recognition technology to:

- **Identify Songs**: Recognize music playing around you
- **Discover New Music**: Find and save songs you hear
- **Build Your Library**: Instantly add identified tracks
- **Access Song Info**: Get complete track, artist, and album details

<Info>
  Musika's audio recognition is powered by [Music Recognizer](https://github.com/aleksey-saenko/MusicRecognizer), providing reliable identification for millions of songs.
</Info>

## How to Use

### Identifying a Song

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Recognition">
    Tap the audio recognition button (waveform or microphone icon) in the app
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Start Listening">
    Hold your device near the audio source for 5-10 seconds
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Results">
    Musika displays the identified song with full details
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Take Action">
    Play, save, or add the song to your library
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  For best results, hold your device close to the audio source and minimize background noise. The recognition needs a clear sample of the music to identify it accurately.
</Tip>

### Quick Recognition

**Fast Identification Process**:
1. Open Musika
2. Tap the recognition button
3. Wait 5-10 seconds while listening
4. Get instant results

<Note>
  Recognition works best with the original studio recording. Live performances, covers, or low-quality recordings may be harder to identify or may match to the original version instead.
</Note>

## Technical Details

### How It Works

Audio recognition uses a sophisticated process:

1. **Audio Capture**: Records a short sample from your microphone
2. **Fingerprinting**: Creates a unique "fingerprint" of the audio
3. **Matching**: Compares fingerprint against massive song database
4. **Results**: Returns matching songs with confidence scores

### Recognition Technology

<Info>
  Musika uses acoustic fingerprinting technology that:
  - Works even with background noise
  - Identifies songs in seconds
  - Handles various audio qualities
  - Maintains privacy (only sends fingerprint, not audio)
</Info>

**Technology Features**:
- **Noise Resistant**: Works in moderately noisy environments
- **Fast Processing**: Results typically in 5-10 seconds
- **High Accuracy**: Reliable identification for mainstream and indie music
- **Privacy-Focused**: Audio samples processed locally when possible

## Permissions & Privacy

### Required Permissions

Audio recognition requires:

- **Microphone Access**: To listen to environmental audio
- **Internet Connection**: To query the music database
- **Storage** (optional): To cache recognition results

<Warning>
  Musika only accesses your microphone when you actively trigger audio recognition. The microphone is never used in the background or without your explicit action.
</Warning>

### Privacy Considerations

**How Your Data is Protected**:
- **Local Processing**: Audio fingerprinting happens on your device when possible
- **No Audio Storage**: Actual audio is never stored or transmitted
- **Anonymous Queries**: Recognition requests don't include personal information
- **Temporary Samples**: Audio samples are immediately discarded after fingerprinting

## Limitations

### When Recognition May Not Work

<Accordion title="Audio Quality Issues">
  - **Too Noisy**: Excessive background noise can interfere
  - **Too Quiet**: Audio source must be audible
  - **Poor Quality**: Heavily compressed or distorted audio
  - **Multiple Songs**: Playing multiple songs simultaneously
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Content Limitations">
  - **Obscure Music**: Very rare or unreleased tracks may not be in the database
  - **Classical Music**: Some classical pieces may be difficult to identify
  - **Ambient/Instrumental**: Songs without distinctive features
  - **Live Performances**: May match to studio version or not at all
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Technical Constraints">
  - **No Internet**: Requires active internet connection
  - **Short Samples**: Needs at least 5-10 seconds of clear audio
  - **Covers/Remixes**: May identify as original version
  - **Regional Availability**: Some tracks may not be available in your region
</Accordion>

## Troubleshooting

<Accordion title="Recognition Not Working">
  **Can't Identify Song**:
  - Ensure microphone permission is granted
  - Move closer to audio source
  - Reduce background noise
  - Try a different part of the song (chorus often works best)
  - Verify internet connection

  **App Not Listening**:
  - Check microphone permissions in device settings
  - Restart the app
  - Verify microphone works in other apps
  - Update to latest version
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Incorrect Results">
  **Wrong Song Identified**:
  - Try recognizing a different part of the song
  - Ensure only one song is playing
  - Check if it's a cover or remix of the identified song
  - Verify audio source quality

  **Multiple Matches**:
  - Musika shows the most confident match
  - Different versions (live, remix) may all match
  - Select the correct version from results
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Performance Issues">
  **Slow Recognition**:
  - Check internet speed
  - Try again if first attempt times out
  - Clear app cache
  - Ensure stable network connection

  **App Crashes**:
  - Update to latest version
  - Clear app cache and data
  - Check for sufficient device storage
  - Report crash via feedback
</Accordion>
