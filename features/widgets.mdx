Musika provides two powerful widgets that let you control your music and identify songs directly from your home screen without opening the app.

## Music Player Widget

The music player widget gives you quick access to playback controls and now-playing information right on your home screen.

### Features

- **Now Playing Display**: See current song title, artist, and album art
- **Playback Controls**: Play/pause, skip forward, skip backward buttons
- **Album Artwork**: Beautiful album art display
- **Quick Access**: Control music without opening the app
- **Auto-Update**: Automatically updates when songs change

### Adding the Music Player Widget

<Steps>
  <Step title="Long-press on home screen">
    Long-press on an empty space on your home screen to enter widget mode
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Find Musika widgets">
    Scroll through the widget list to find Musika
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Music Player widget">
    Tap the Music Player widget to add it to your home screen
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Position the widget">
    Drag the widget to your desired location and release
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Widget Sizes

The music player widget supports multiple sizes:
- **Small (2x2)**: Compact display with essential controls
- **Medium (4x2)**: Standard size with full track information
- **Large (4x3)**: Extended view with additional information

## Find Widget

The Find widget provides instant song identification directly from your home screen using audio recognition technology.

### Features

- **Quick Recognition**: Tap to identify songs playing around you
- **One-Tap Access**: Fastest way to identify music
- **Integration**: Works with Shazam's music recognition service
- **Recognition History**: View previously identified songs
- **Direct Playback**: Tap identified songs to play them

### Adding the Find Widget

<Steps>
  <Step title="Long-press on home screen">
    Long-press on an empty space on your home screen
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Find Musika widgets">
    Scroll to find Musika in the widget list
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Find widget">
    Tap the Find widget to add it
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Position the widget">
    Place the widget where you want it on your home screen
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Using the Find Widget

1. **Tap the widget** when music is playing
2. **Grant microphone permission** if prompted
3. **Wait for recognition** (usually 2-5 seconds)
4. **View results** and tap to play the song in Musika

<Note>
  The Find widget requires microphone permission to record audio for song identification.
</Note>

## Widget Customization

While widgets inherit your app theme, you can customize their behavior:

- **Auto-refresh**: Widgets automatically update with playback changes
- **Theme Integration**: Widgets match your app's dark/light theme
- **Battery Optimization**: Widgets are designed for minimal battery impact

## Troubleshooting

<Accordion title="Widget not updating">
  If your widget isn't showing current playback:
  
  1. Remove and re-add the widget
  2. Check battery optimization settings for Musika
  3. Ensure Musika has permission to run in background
  4. Restart your device
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Find widget not working">
  If song recognition fails:
  
  1. Verify microphone permission is granted
  2. Check internet connection (required for recognition)
  3. Ensure music is audible and not too quiet
  4. Try again in a quieter environment
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Controls not responding">
  If widget buttons don't work:
  
  1. Ensure Musika is running or was recently active
  2. Check if app has been force-stopped
  3. Remove and re-add the widget
  4. Verify playback service is running
</Accordion>
