## Creating Playlists

### Local Playlists

Create playlists stored on your device:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start Creation">
    Tap the "New Playlist" button in your Library
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Name Your Playlist">
    Enter a playlist name and optional description
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Songs">
    Search and add tracks to your new playlist
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Customize">
    Set playlist artwork and metadata
  </Step>
</Steps>

### YouTube Music Playlists

Create playlists that sync with YouTube Music:

<Info>
  Playlists created in Musika can be synced to your YouTube Music account, making them accessible across all your devices and the YouTube Music app.
</Info>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable Sync">
    Sign in to your YouTube account in Settings
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create with Sync">
    Enable "Sync to YouTube Music" when creating a playlist
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Tracks">
    Add songs - they'll sync to YouTube Music automatically
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Access Everywhere">
    Playlist appears in YouTube Music and other devices
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Editing Playlists

### Managing Content

<Accordion title="Adding Songs">
  **Multiple Ways to Add**:
  - From Now Playing screen: Tap menu > Add to Playlist
  - From search results: Long-press track > Add to Playlist
  - From albums: Select tracks > Add to Playlist
  - Drag and drop from library

  **Bulk Adding**:
  - Select multiple tracks at once
  - Add entire albums to playlists
  - Import from other playlists
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Removing Songs">
  **Remove Individual Tracks**:
  - Swipe left on track (if enabled)
  - Long-press > Remove from Playlist
  - Edit mode > Select > Delete

  **Bulk Removal**:
  - Enter edit mode
  - Select multiple tracks
  - Tap Delete button
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Reordering">
  **Manual Reordering**:
  - Long-press and drag tracks to new positions
  - Use move handles in edit mode
  - Cut/paste tracks to different positions

  **Auto-Sorting**:
  - Sort by title, artist, or album
  - Sort by date added
  - Sort by play count or rating
  - Shuffle playlist order
</Accordion>

### Playlist Metadata

Customize playlist information:

- **Name**: Edit playlist title
- **Description**: Add detailed description
- **Artwork**: Set custom cover image from:
  - Device photos
  - Album art from tracks
  - Default generated artwork
- **Privacy**: Set public or private (YouTube Music playlists)

<Tip>
  Use descriptive names and artwork to make playlists easily identifiable. Descriptions help you remember why you created the playlist.
</Tip>

### Syncing Playlists

Keep playlists synchronized with YouTube Music:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Download from YouTube" icon="download">
    Pull your YouTube Music playlists into Musika
  </Card>
  <Card title="Upload to YouTube" icon="upload">
    Push Musika playlists to YouTube Music
  </Card>
  <Card title="Two-Way Sync" icon="arrows-rotate">

  </Card>

### Sync Settings

<Accordion title="Automatic Sync">
  - **Real-time sync**: Changes sync immediately when online
  - **Periodic sync**: Sync at regular intervals
  - **Manual sync**: Only sync when you trigger it
  - **WiFi-only**: Limit sync to WiFi connections
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Selective Sync">
  - Choose which playlists to sync
  - Sync all or specific playlists
  - Exclude playlists from sync
  - One-way sync options (download-only or upload-only)
</Accordion>

<Note>
  Changes made to synced playlists in either Musika or YouTube Music will be reflected in both apps after the next sync.
</Note>

## Smart Playlists

### Auto-Generated Playlists

Musika creates dynamic playlists based on your listening:

- **Quick Picks**: AI-powered recommendations based on your taste
- **Recently Played**: Automatically tracks your listening history
- **Most Played**: Your most frequently played tracks
- **Recently Added**: Newly added music to your library
- **Favorites**: Based on likes and play counts

### Custom Smart Playlists

Create playlists that update automatically based on criteria:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Smart Playlist">
    Select "New Smart Playlist" from menu
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Rules">
    Define criteria (genre, play count, date added, etc.)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure Limits">
    Set maximum number of tracks or duration
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Auto-Update">
    Playlist updates automatically as your library changes
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Smart playlists are dynamic - they automatically add and remove tracks based on your rules without manual intervention.
</Info>

## Import & Export

### Backup Playlists

Protect your playlists with backup and restore:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Export Playlists">
    Settings > Playlists > Export
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose Format">
    Select export format (M3U, JSON, or Musika format)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Location">
    Save to device storage or cloud storage
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Restore Later">
    Import the file to restore playlists on any device
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Import from Other Services

Bring playlists from other music services:

- **Spotify**: Import Spotify playlists (via playlist URL)
- **Apple Music**: Import M3U exports
- **Other Services**: Import standard playlist formats
- **YouTube**: Import YouTube Music playlists directly

<Warning>
  When importing from other services, track availability depends on YouTube Music's catalog. Some tracks may not be found or may have different versions.
</Warning>

## Troubleshooting

<Accordion title="Sync Issues">
  **Playlists Not Syncing**:
  - Check internet connection
  - Verify YouTube account is signed in
  - Manually trigger sync from Settings
  - Check sync settings for that playlist

  **Missing Tracks After Sync**:
  - Tracks may not be available in YouTube Music
  - Check for region restrictions
  - Verify tracks weren't removed from YouTube Music
  - Try re-adding the missing tracks
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Playback Problems">
  **Playlist Won't Play**:
  - Verify tracks are available
  - Check internet connection for non-downloaded content
  - Try removing and re-adding problematic tracks
  - Clear app cache

  **Songs Skipping**:
  - Some tracks may be unavailable or region-locked
  - Check if tracks need to be downloaded for offline
  - Verify YouTube Music account status
</Accordion>
