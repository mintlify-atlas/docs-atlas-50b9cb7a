## Viewing Lyrics

### Access Lyrics

<Steps>
  <Step title="Play a Song">
    Start playing any track in your library or from YouTube Music
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Open Lyrics">
    Tap the lyrics button on the Now Playing screen
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Watch Sync">
    Lyrics automatically highlight and scroll in sync with the music
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Interact">
    Tap any line to jump to that part of the song
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Swipe up on the Now Playing screen to expand lyrics view, or tap the fullscreen button for an immersive experience.
</Tip>

### Lyrics Display Modes

<Accordion title="Standard View">
  - Lyrics appear below album artwork on Now Playing screen
  - Shows 3-4 lines at a time
  - Automatic scrolling with current line highlighted
  - Tap to expand or collapse
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Fullscreen View">
  - Dedicates entire screen to lyrics
  - Larger, more readable text
  - Enhanced visual highlighting
  - Perfect for karaoke or sing-alongs
  - Album artwork shown as blurred background
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Mini View">
  - Single line of current lyrics
  - Minimal screen space
  - Shows in notification (if enabled)
  - Quick glance at current line
</Accordion>

## Synchronized Lyrics

### Real-Time Synchronization

Musika's synced lyrics provide frame-accurate synchronization:

- **Line-by-Line**: Each line highlights precisely when sung
- **Word-Level** (when available): Individual words highlight in real-time
- **Auto-Scroll**: Lyrics automatically scroll to keep current line visible
- **Smooth Transitions**: Seamless highlighting without jarring jumps

### Interactive Controls

<Info>
  Tap any lyrics line to instantly jump to that point in the song. Perfect for learning specific parts or revisiting favorite verses.
</Info>

**Interactive Features**:
- **Tap to Seek**: Jump to any part of the song
- **Manual Scrolling**: Scroll ahead to see upcoming lyrics
- **Auto-Resume**: Returns to auto-scroll when playback position changes
- **Search Within Lyrics**: Find specific words or phrases

## Lyrics Sources

### Multi-Source Fetching

Musika searches multiple sources to find the best lyrics:

<Accordion title="LRCLIB">
  - **Primary Source**: Community-contributed synchronized lyrics
  - **High Quality**: Professionally timed lyrics with word-level sync
  - **Extensive Coverage**: Millions of songs across all genres
  - **Accurate Timing**: Precise synchronization data
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="YouTube">
  - **Official Lyrics**: Lyrics provided by YouTube Music
  - **Auto-Generated**: YouTube's automatic transcription when available
  - **Fallback Source**: Used when LRCLIB doesn't have lyrics
  - **Wide Coverage**: Available for most YouTube Music tracks
</Accordion>

<Note>
  Musika automatically selects the best available lyrics source. If both are available, it prioritizes LRCLIB for better synchronization quality.
</Note>

### Translation Quality

AI translation provides:

- **Natural Phrasing**: Translations sound natural, not robotic
- **Cultural Context**: Understands cultural references and idioms
- **Poetic Preservation**: Attempts to maintain poetic qualities when possible
- **Slang Recognition**: Handles modern slang and colloquialisms

<Info>

</Info>

## Lyrics Search

### Search Within Lyrics

Find specific words or phrases in the current song's lyrics:

- **Instant Search**: Type to find matching lyrics lines
- **Highlight Matches**: All matching lines are highlighted
- **Jump to Match**: Tap result to jump to that part of the song
- **Case-Insensitive**: Finds matches regardless of capitalization

### Search by Lyrics

Find songs by remembered lyrics:

1. Open search in Musika
2. Enter lyrics fragment you remember
3. Results show songs containing those lyrics
4. Tap to play and see full lyrics

<Tip>
  Can't remember the song name? Search by any phrase you remember - Musika will find songs with matching lyrics.
</Tip>

## Lyrics Settings

### Configuration Options

Customize lyrics behavior in Settings > Lyrics:

- **Auto-Show Lyrics**: Automatically display lyrics when available
- **Preferred Source**: Choose default lyrics source
- **Translation Language**: Set default translation language
- **Font Size**: Adjust lyrics text size
- **Show in Notification**: Display current line in notification
- **Fullscreen by Default**: Always open in fullscreen mode

<Note>
  Settings are per-device and don't sync across devices. Configure each device to your preferences.
</Note>

## Troubleshooting

<Accordion title="No Lyrics Available">
  **Lyrics Not Found**:
  - Song may be too new or obscure
  - Try alternate versions or covers of the song
  - Check if lyrics are available on LRCLIB.net
  - Submit lyrics to LRCLIB to help the community

  **Lyrics Won't Load**:
  - Check internet connection
  - Verify app has network permissions
  - Clear app cache
  - Update to latest version
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Synchronization Issues">
  **Lyrics Out of Sync**:
  - Different audio version may cause timing issues
  - Try refreshing lyrics (pull down to refresh)
  - Report timing issues on LRCLIB
  - Manually adjust sync offset in settings (coming soon)

  **Lyrics Lag or Stutter**:
  - Close other apps to free memory
  - Disable word-level sync for better performance
  - Reduce lyrics text size
  - Restart the app
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Translation Problems">
  **Translation Not Working**:
  - Requires internet connection
  - Check if AI features are enabled
  - Verify you're using full version (not FOSS)
  - Update to latest version

  **Translation Quality Issues**:
  - Some language pairs may have better quality than others
  - Complex metaphors may not translate well
  - Try different translation services in settings
  - Report issues for improvement
</Accordion>
