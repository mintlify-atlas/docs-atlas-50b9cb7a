Musika's sleep timer allows you to enjoy music while falling asleep without worrying about it playing all night. Set a timer and let the music gently fade out, helping you drift off peacefully.

## Overview

The sleep timer automatically stops playback after a specified duration, perfect for:

- **Bedtime Listening**: Fall asleep to music without draining battery
- **Meditation Sessions**: Set timed sessions for meditation or relaxation
- **Focus Work**: Time-box listening sessions during work or study
- **Power Naps**: Wake up to silence after a short rest
- **Podcasts**: Stop playback after current episode or chapter

<Info>
  Unlike simple timers, Musika's sleep timer includes fade-out functionality, gradually reducing volume for a smooth transition to silence.
</Info>

## Setting a Sleep Timer

### Quick Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start Playback">

  </Step>

  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>

</Tip>

## Fade-Out Feature

### Gradual Volume Reduction

Instead of abruptly stopping, the sleep timer includes a gentle fade-out:

- **Smooth Transition**: Volume gradually decreases over final minutes
- **Peaceful Stop**: Music fades to silence naturally
- **Customizable Duration**: Configure fade-out length in settings
- **Natural Feel**: Mimics the way you naturally drift to sleep

<Accordion title="How Fade-Out Works">
  When enabled, the sleep timer begins reducing volume during the final minutes:
  
  - **Default**: 2-minute fade-out starting before timer expires
  - **Configurable**: Adjust fade duration from 30 seconds to 5 minutes
  - **Smooth Curve**: Volume reduces on a logarithmic curve for natural feel
  - **Gentle Stop**: Playback stops only after reaching zero volume
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Enable/Disable Fade-Out">
  Configure fade-out behavior:
  
  1. Go to Settings > Playback > Sleep Timer
  2. Toggle "Fade Out" option
  3. Adjust "Fade Duration" slider
  4. Changes apply to all future timers
  
  **Options**:
  - Enable fade-out with custom duration
  - Disable for instant stop
  - Adjust fade curve (linear, logarithmic, exponential)
</Accordion>

## Smart Timer Features

### End of Track Option

<Info>
  Enable "Finish Current Track" to let the current song complete before stopping, even if the timer expires during playback.
</Info>

**How It Works**:
- Timer expires during a song
- Instead of stopping mid-song, playback continues
- Playback stops (with fade-out) after track ends
- Perfect for avoiding jarring interruptions

### End of Queue Option

Stop after all queued tracks complete:

- Timer acts as maximum duration
- Playback continues until queue ends or timer expires
- Useful for playlist-based sleep music
- Ensures you hear complete content

<Tip>
  Combine "End of Track" with a short timer to ensure the current song finishes without playing much more.
</Tip>

## Timer Management

### Active Timer Display

When a timer is active, you'll see:

- **Countdown Display**: Remaining time shown on Now Playing screen

- **Time Remaining**: Updated every minute

### Modify Active Timer

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Timer">
    Tap the timer display or menu button
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose Action">
    - Extend timer (add more time)
    - Reduce timer (less time)
    - Change duration completely
    - Cancel timer
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm Changes">

  </Step>
</Steps>

### Cancel Timer

Stop the timer before it expires:

- Tap timer countdown on Now Playing screen
- Select "Cancel Timer" from menu
- Or set timer duration to 0
- Playback continues normally

### Optimal Settings for Sleep

- **Duration**: 30-45 minutes (most people fall asleep within this time)
- **Fade-Out**: 2-3 minutes (gradual enough to be unnoticeable)
- **Finish Track**: Enabled (prevents mid-song stops)
- **Reminder**: Disabled (won't wake you if you've fallen asleep)

<Tip>
  Create a dedicated "Sleep" playlist with calming music that's longer than your timer duration. This ensures varied music each night within your timer window.
</Tip>

## Troubleshooting

<Accordion title="Timer Not Working">
  **Timer Doesn't Stop Playback**:
  - Verify timer is actually set (check notification)
  - Ensure app isn't being killed by battery optimization
  - Check timer didn't get canceled accidentally
  - Update to latest version
  - Restart app and try again

  **Timer Stops Immediately**:
  - Check if duration was set to very short time
  - Verify system time is correct
  - Clear app cache
  - Reinstall if issue persists
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Fade-Out Issues">
  **No Fade-Out Occurring**:
  - Verify fade-out is enabled in settings
  - Check fade duration isn't set to 0
  - Ensure timer duration is longer than fade duration
  - Test fade-out independently

  **Fade Too Sudden or Too Long**:
  - Adjust fade duration in settings
  - Try different fade curve types
  - Match fade duration to your preferences (1-3 min typical)
  - Test with different music volumes
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Notification Problems">
  **Not Receiving Notifications**:
  - Check notification permissions for Musika
  - Verify notifications enabled in Sleep Timer settings
  - Check Do Not Disturb settings
  - Ensure notification channel isn't disabled

  **Too Many Notifications**:
  - Disable unwanted notifications in settings
  - Adjust reminder timing
  - Use less frequent reminder intervals
  - Disable timer start notifications
</Accordion>
