Musika tracks your listening activity to provide insights into your music habits, help you rediscover songs, and create personalized year-end summaries.

## Listening History

Musika maintains a complete history of every song you've played, making it easy to find and replay tracks you've recently enjoyed.

### Features

- **Complete Playback Log**: Every song you play is recorded
- **Chronological Timeline**: See when you listened to each track
- **Quick Replay**: Tap any song in history to play it again
- **Search History**: Find specific songs you've played before
- **Date Filtering**: Browse history by date range
- **Persistent Storage**: History is saved locally on your device

### Accessing Your History

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open History screen">
    Navigate to **Library** → **History** or access from the main menu
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Browse your timeline">
    Scroll through your listening history in reverse chronological order
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Play from history">
    Tap any song to play it again, or long-press for more options
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Managing History

- **Clear History**: Remove all playback history
- **Delete Individual Items**: Remove specific songs from history
- **Export History**: Back up your listening data
- **Privacy Mode**: Temporarily disable history tracking

<Info>
  History is stored locally on your device. If you clear app data or reinstall, your history will be lost unless backed up.
</Info>

## Statistics

Get detailed insights into your listening patterns with comprehensive statistics about your music consumption.

### Available Statistics

**Most Played**
- Top songs by play count
- Most played artists
- Favorite albums
- Top genres

**Listening Time**
- Total time spent listening
- Average daily listening time
- Listening time by day/week/month
- Time spent per artist/album

**Discovery**
- New artists discovered
- New songs added to library
- Playlist growth over time

**Trends**
- Listening patterns by time of day
- Weekly listening trends
- Seasonal preferences

### Viewing Statistics

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Stats screen">
    Navigate to **Library** → **Stats** from the main menu
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select time period">
    Choose from:
    - Last 7 days
    - Last 30 days
    - Last 3 months
    - Last 6 months
    - Last year
    - All time
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Explore your data">
    View charts, lists, and insights about your listening habits
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Top Charts

Musika generates personal charts based on your listening:

- **Top 10 Songs**: Your most played tracks
- **Top Artists**: Artists you listen to most
- **Top Albums**: Most played albums
- **Rising Artists**: Artists you're listening to more recently

## Wrapped

At the end of each year, Musika creates a personalized "Wrapped" summary of your listening year, similar to popular streaming services.

### Wrapped Features

**Your Year in Music**
- Total minutes listened
- Number of different songs played
- Number of artists discovered
- Top songs of the year
- Top artists of the year
- Top albums of the year
- Most listened-to genre

**Listening Milestones**
- First song of the year
- Most repeated song
- Longest listening session
- Discovery achievements

**Personal Insights**
- Listening personality analysis
- Genre diversity score
- Music explorer level
- Favorite season for music

### Accessing Wrapped

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Wrapped">
    Navigate to **Library** → **Wrapped** (available each December)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View your summary">
    Swipe through your personalized music year summary
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Share your Wrapped">
    Share your stats on social media or with friends
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Wrapped is generated at the end of each year (typically in December) based on your listening from January to November.
</Note>

## Play Counts

Every song, album, and artist in your library displays play count statistics.

### Track-Level Counts

- **Total Plays**: How many times you've played each song
- **Last Played**: When you last listened to a song
- **Play Streak**: Consecutive days with plays
- **All-Time Ranking**: How songs rank against each other

### Using Play Counts

**Smart Playlists**  
Create playlists based on play counts:
- Top 100 most played
- Recently played
- Never played (new discoveries)
- One-hit wonders (played once)

**Sorting**  
Sort your library by:
- Most played
- Least played
- Recently played
- Never played

### Resetting Play Counts

You can reset play counts if desired:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Privacy**
2. Tap **Reset Play Counts**
3. Confirm the action

<Warning>
  Resetting play counts is permanent and cannot be undone. Your history and Wrapped data will also be affected.
</Warning>

## Privacy Controls

Control what data Musika tracks:

### History Settings

- **Enable/Disable History**: Turn playback history on or off
- **Auto-Delete**: Automatically remove history older than X days
- **Pause History**: Temporarily stop tracking without deleting data

### Statistics Settings

- **Anonymous Statistics**: Collect stats without identifying information
- **Play Count Tracking**: Enable or disable play count increments
- **Disable Wrapped**: Opt out of year-end summary generation

### Data Export

Export your listening data:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Privacy** → **Export Data**
2. Choose what to export:
   - Listening history
   - Play counts
   - Statistics
   - Wrapped summaries
3. Select export format (CSV, JSON)
4. Save file to device or share

<Tip>
  Regular data exports serve as backups if you need to reinstall the app or switch devices.
</Tip>
