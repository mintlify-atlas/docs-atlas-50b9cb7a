## Playback Features

### Basic Controls
- **Standard Controls**: Play, pause, skip forward/backward, seek, and volume control
- **Shuffle & Repeat**: Randomize track order or repeat single tracks/playlists
- **Background Playback**: Listen while using other apps or with screen off
- **Lock Screen Controls**: Full playback controls accessible from lock screen

### Advanced Playback

<Accordion title="Crossfade & Gapless Playback">
  - **Crossfade** (Beta): Smooth transitions between tracks for seamless listening
  - **Gapless Playback**: Eliminates silence between tracks for continuous albums
  - **Audio Effects**: Built-in equalizer and audio enhancements
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Playback Speed & Pitch Control">
  - **Variable Speed**: Adjust playback from 0.5x to 2.0x speed
  - **Independent Pitch**: Modify audio pitch without affecting speed
  - Perfect for learning songs or changing the listening experience
</Accordion>

## Discovery & Search

### Smart Search
- **Universal Search**: Search across all content types in one place
- **Voice Search**: Voice-activated search functionality
- **Search History**: Quick access to previous searches
- **Auto-complete**: Real-time search suggestions as you type
- **Advanced Filters**: Filter results by type, date, and more

### Content Discovery

<Info>
  Musika provides personalized music discovery through multiple channels:
  - **Home Feed**: AI-powered personalized recommendations
  - **Trending**: Currently popular music and artists
  - **Charts**: Top tracks and artists by region
  - **New Releases**: Latest music releases from your favorite artists
  - **Moods & Genres**: Music organized by mood and genre categories
</Info>

## System Integration

### Media Controls
- **Media Session**: Integration with system media controls
- **Notification Controls**: Rich media notifications with artwork and controls
- **Android Auto**: Full support for in-car listening
- **Widget Support**: Home screen widgets for quick playback access

### Casting & Streaming
- **Chromecast**: Cast to Chromecast-enabled devices
- **DLNA/UPnP**: Stream to network speakers, Smart TVs, and media renderers
- **Bluetooth**: Enhanced Bluetooth audio support
