## Getting Started

Musika is a powerful music streaming app that connects to YouTube Music, giving you access to millions of songs, live performances, remixes, and more. This guide will help you get started with the basics.

## Basic Playback Controls

Musika provides all the essential playback controls you need to enjoy your music.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Play/Pause">
    Tap the play button to start playback or pause to temporarily stop the music. The button toggles between play and pause states.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Skip Tracks">
    Use the skip forward and skip backward buttons to navigate through your queue. Long press to seek within the current track.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Adjust Volume">
    Control playback volume using the volume slider or your device's physical volume buttons.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Playback Modes

### Shuffle Mode

Randomize the order of tracks in your queue:

1. Tap the shuffle icon in the Now Playing screen
2. When enabled, tracks will play in random order
3. Tap again to disable shuffle and return to sequential playback

### Repeat Mode

Control how playback repeats:

- **Repeat Off**: Playback stops after the queue ends
- **Repeat All**: The entire queue repeats continuously
- **Repeat One**: The current track repeats indefinitely

Tap the repeat icon to cycle through these modes.

## Advanced Playback Features

<Note>
  Some advanced features may be in beta and could behave differently across devices.
</Note>

### Gapless Playback

Musika supports gapless playback for seamless transitions between tracks, perfect for albums and DJ mixes.

### Audio Effects

Access the built-in equalizer and audio enhancements:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Audio Settings">
    Navigate to Settings > Audio from the app menu.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enable Equalizer">
    Toggle on the equalizer and choose from preset profiles or create your own custom settings.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Adjust Audio Effects">
    Fine-tune bass boost, virtualizer, and other audio enhancements to your preference.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Playback Speed Control

Adjust playback speed from 0.5x to 2.0x:

1. Open the Now Playing screen
2. Tap the playback speed icon
3. Select your desired speed or use the slider for precise control

<Tip>
  Playback speed is great for podcasts or learning music by ear!
</Tip>

## Background Playback

Musika continues playing music even when the app is minimized or your screen is locked.

### Lock Screen Controls

When music is playing, you'll see full playback controls on your lock screen:

- Album artwork
- Song title and artist
- Play/pause, skip forward, and skip backward buttons
- Progress bar with seek capability

### Notification Controls

The persistent notification provides quick access to:

- Current track information
- Playback controls
- Album artwork
- Quick actions (like, add to playlist)

<Note>
  To ensure uninterrupted playback, make sure Musika has permission to run in the background and isn't being battery optimized by your device.
</Note>

## Discovering Music

### Home Feed

Your personalized home feed shows:

- Recommended music based on your listening history
- Quick picks selected just for you
- Recently played tracks and albums
- Trending music and new releases

### Browse Categories

Explore music through different categories:

- **Charts**: Popular music charts by region
- **Moods & Genres**: Music organized by mood and genre
- **New Releases**: The latest music releases
- **Podcasts**: Audio podcast content

### Search

Find exactly what you're looking for:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Search">
    Tap the search icon in the top navigation bar.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Query">
    Type your search query or use voice search for hands-free searching.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Filter Results">
    Use the filter options to narrow results by type (songs, albums, artists, playlists).
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Your search history is saved for quick access to frequently searched terms.
</Tip>

## Sleep Timer

Set Musika to automatically stop playback after a specified duration:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Sleep Timer">
    Tap the menu icon (three dots) in the Now Playing screen and select Sleep Timer.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Duration">
    Choose a preset duration or set a custom time.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enable Fade Out (Optional)">
    Enable fade out to gradually reduce volume before playback stops.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm">
    Tap Set to activate the sleep timer. A notification will remind you before it expires.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Lyrics

View synchronized lyrics while listening:

1. Tap the lyrics icon in the Now Playing screen
2. Lyrics will scroll automatically in sync with the music
3. Tap any line to jump to that part of the song
4. Swipe up for fullscreen lyrics view

<Note>
  Lyrics are sourced from LRCLIB and YouTube. If lyrics aren't available for a song, the button will be disabled.
</Note>

## Troubleshooting

### Playback Issues

If you experience playback problems:

- Check your internet connection
- Clear the app cache in Settings > Storage
- Restart the app
- Ensure you're not in data saver mode if on mobile data

### Background Playback Stops

If playback stops when you minimize the app:

1. Go to your device Settings > Apps > Musika
2. Disable battery optimization for Musika
3. Enable "Allow background activity"
4. Ensure notification permissions are granted

### Poor Audio Quality

To improve audio quality:

- Connect to WiFi instead of cellular data
- Check streaming quality settings in the app
- Disable data saver mode
- Ensure your internet connection is stable
