## Overview

Musika offers extensive customization options to personalize your music experience. From themes and visual settings to playback preferences and advanced configurations, you can tailor the app to your exact needs.

## Theme Settings

### Dark Theme

Musika features a modern dark theme designed for comfortable viewing:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Tap the menu icon and navigate to Settings.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Appearance">
    Go to Appearance > Theme.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Theme Mode">
    Select your preferred theme:
    - **Dark**: Eye-friendly dark mode
    - **Light**: Bright, clean interface
    - **System Default**: Follow your device's system theme
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Musika uses Material Design 3 principles for a modern, cohesive user experience across all themes.
</Note>

### Color Customization

While Musika's current design focuses on the dark theme, the Material Design 3 foundation supports:

- Dynamic color schemes based on album artwork
- Consistent iconography throughout the app
- Typography using the custom Poppins font family

## Display Settings

### Now Playing Screen

Customize how the Now Playing screen looks and behaves:

1. Go to Settings > Appearance > Now Playing
2. Configure options:
   - **Show Album Artwork**: Display full-screen album artwork
   - **Blur Background**: Apply blur effect to artwork background
   - **Show Lyrics Button**: Toggle visibility of lyrics button
   - **Show Queue Button**: Toggle visibility of queue button

### Typography

Musika uses the Poppins font family for a clean, modern look:

- Consistent typography across all screens
- Optimized text sizes for readability
- Clear visual hierarchy

### Screen Orientation

Configure how the app handles screen rotation:

1. Go to Settings > Display
2. Choose orientation preference:
   - **Portrait**: Lock to portrait mode
   - **Landscape**: Lock to landscape mode
   - **Auto-rotate**: Follow device orientation settings

<Tip>
  Landscape mode is especially useful on tablets for a more immersive Now Playing experience.
</Tip>

## Audio Settings

### Equalizer

Fine-tune audio output with the built-in equalizer:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Audio Settings">
    Go to Settings > Audio > Equalizer.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enable Equalizer">
    Toggle the equalizer on.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Preset">
    Select from built-in presets:
    - Normal
    - Classical
    - Dance
    - Pop
    - Rock
    - Jazz
    - And more
  </Step>

  <Step title="Custom Settings (Optional)">
    Create your own custom EQ by adjusting individual frequency bands.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Audio Effects

Enhance your listening experience with additional audio effects:

- **Bass Boost**: Enhance low-frequency response
- **Virtualizer**: Create a surround sound effect
- **Loudness Enhancer**: Increase overall volume and dynamics

<Warning>
  Extreme audio effect settings may cause distortion. Start with subtle adjustments and increase gradually.
</Warning>

### Crossfade

Enable smooth transitions between tracks:

1. Go to Settings > Audio > Crossfade
2. Enable crossfade
3. Adjust crossfade duration (1-12 seconds)

<Note>
  Crossfade is currently in beta and may behave differently across devices.
</Note>

### Gapless Playback

Enable seamless playback for albums and mixes:

1. Go to Settings > Audio
2. Toggle "Gapless Playback" on

<Tip>
  Gapless playback is perfect for concept albums, live recordings, and DJ mixes.
</Tip>

## Playback Settings

### Audio Quality

Set streaming quality based on your network:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Audio Quality Settings">
    Go to Settings > Audio > Streaming Quality.
  </Step>

  <Step title="WiFi Quality">
    Set quality when connected to WiFi:
    - Low (64 kbps)
    - Normal (128 kbps)
    - High (256 kbps)
    - Best (320 kbps)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Mobile Data Quality">
    Set quality when on cellular data to manage data usage.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Data Saver Mode

Reduce data usage while streaming:

1. Go to Settings > Audio
2. Enable "Data Saver Mode"
3. Streaming quality will automatically adjust to conserve data

### Playback Speed

Configure default playback speed settings:

1. Go to Settings > Playback
2. Set default playback speed (0.5x - 2.0x)
3. Enable "Remember Playback Speed" to maintain speed across sessions

## Notification Settings

### Media Notifications

Customize playback notifications:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Notification Settings">
    Go to Settings > Notifications.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure Display">
    Choose what appears in notifications:
    - Album artwork
    - Song title and artist
    - Playback controls
    - Progress bar
  </Step>

  <Step title="Notification Actions">
    Select quick actions to display:
    - Like/Unlike
    - Add to playlist
    - Share
    - Download
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Lock Screen Controls

Customize lock screen media controls:

1. Go to Settings > Notifications > Lock Screen
2. Enable "Show on Lock Screen"
3. Choose control visibility and style

## Navigation Settings

### Bottom Navigation

Customize which tabs appear in the bottom navigation:

1. Go to Settings > Interface > Navigation
2. Select tabs to display (up to 5):
   - Home
   - Search
   - Library
   - Charts
   - Podcasts
3. Drag to reorder tabs

### Gesture Controls

Enable and configure gesture navigation:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Gesture Settings">
    Go to Settings > Interface > Gestures.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enable Gestures">
    Toggle on gesture controls.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure Actions">
    Customize swipe actions:
    - Swipe left on song: Add to queue / Add to playlist
    - Swipe right on song: Like / Download
    - Swipe down in Now Playing: Minimize player
    - Swipe up in Now Playing: Show queue
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Widget Settings

Customize the home screen widget:

1. Long-press the Musika widget on your home screen
2. Tap "Widget Settings"
3. Configure:
   - Size (small, medium, large)
   - Background transparency
   - Control visibility
   - Update frequency

<Note>
  Widget customization options may vary depending on your Android version and launcher.
</Note>

## Account Settings

### Multiple Accounts

Musika supports multiple YouTube Music accounts:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Account Settings">
    Go to Settings > Accounts.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Account">
    Tap "Add Account" and sign in with your YouTube credentials.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Switch Accounts">
    Tap your profile icon to quickly switch between accounts.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Guest Mode

Use Musika without signing in:

1. Go to Settings > Accounts
2. Select "Use as Guest"
3. Browse and stream music without account sync

<Warning>
  Guest mode provides limited functionality. Playlists, history, and preferences won't be saved.
</Warning>

## Storage Settings

### Cache Management

Control how Musika uses cache:

<Steps>
  <Step title="View Cache Usage">
    Go to Settings > Storage to see current cache size.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Cache Limit">
    Configure maximum cache size (100 MB - 5 GB).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Clear Cache">
    Tap "Clear Cache" to free up space immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Download Location

Choose where downloads are stored:

1. Go to Settings > Storage > Download Location
2. Select:
   - Internal Storage
   - SD Card (if available)
   - Custom folder

## AI Translation Settings

Configure AI-powered lyrics translation:

### Using OpenRouter (Recommended)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get API Key">
    Sign up and obtain an API key from [OpenRouter](https://openrouter.ai/).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open AI Settings">
    Go to Settings > AI Settings.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Provider">
    Ensure "Provider" is set to "OpenRouter".
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter API Key">
    Paste your OpenRouter API key.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Using Custom Provider

For OpenAI, Anthropic, or local LLMs:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open AI Settings">
    Go to Settings > AI Settings.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Provider">
    Choose your provider (ChatGPT, Gemini, or Custom).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure Base URL (Custom Only)">
    If using Custom, enter your provider's base URL.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter API Key">
    Provide your API key for the selected service.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  AI translation allows you to translate lyrics to your preferred language in real-time.
</Tip>

## Advanced Settings

### Android Auto

Configure Musika for Android Auto:

1. Go to Settings > Android Auto
2. Enable Android Auto integration
3. Configure:
   - Queue behavior
   - Voice command support
   - Display preferences

### Network Settings

Optimize network behavior:

- **Prefer WiFi**: Automatically pause streaming when WiFi disconnects
- **Connection Timeout**: Adjust timeout for slow connections
- **Retry Failed Streams**: Automatically retry failed streams

### Performance Settings

Optimize app performance:

1. Go to Settings > Advanced > Performance
2. Configure:
   - **Fast Startup**: Preload content on app launch
   - **Smooth Scrolling**: Enable hardware acceleration
   - **Memory Management**: Adjust memory usage limits
   - **Battery Optimization**: Balance performance and battery life

## Resetting Settings

### Reset Individual Settings

Reset specific setting categories:

1. Go to the relevant settings section
2. Tap "Reset to Default"
3. Confirm reset

### Reset All Settings

<Warning>
  Resetting all settings will return Musika to default configuration. Your library, playlists, and downloads will not be affected.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Advanced Settings">
    Go to Settings > Advanced.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Reset">
    Tap "Reset All Settings".
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm">
    Confirm that you want to reset all settings to defaults.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Restart App">
    Restart Musika for changes to take full effect.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tips for Optimal Experience

### Performance

- Clear cache regularly if experiencing slow performance
- Limit cache size on devices with limited storage
- Enable hardware acceleration for smoother scrolling

### Battery Life

- Use dark theme to save battery on OLED screens
- Disable crossfade if not needed
- Lower streaming quality on mobile data
- Enable battery optimization in settings

### Audio Quality

- Use "Best" quality on WiFi for optimal sound
- Enable gapless playback for albums
- Adjust equalizer based on your headphones/speakers
- Use crossfade for party playlists

### Customization Workflow

1. Start with theme and appearance settings
2. Configure audio quality and effects
3. Set up navigation and gestures
4. Customize notifications and widgets
5. Fine-tune advanced settings as needed
