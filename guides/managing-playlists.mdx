## Overview

Playlists are the perfect way to organize your music in Musika. You can create custom playlists, sync with YouTube Music playlists, and organize your favorite tracks exactly how you want.

## Creating Playlists

### Create a Local Playlist

Build custom playlists stored on your device:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Playlist Menu">
    Tap the menu icon and navigate to Library > Playlists.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create New Playlist">
    Tap the "+" or "Create Playlist" button.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Details">
    Provide a name for your playlist. Optionally add a description.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Artwork (Optional)">
    Choose a custom cover image for your playlist or let Musika generate one automatically.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save">
    Tap "Create" to save your new playlist.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Create from Queue

Quickly create a playlist from your current playback queue:

1. Open the Now Playing screen
2. Tap the queue icon to view your current queue
3. Tap the three-dot menu
4. Select "Save Queue as Playlist"
5. Name your playlist and save

<Tip>
  This is perfect for preserving a great listening session or creating a playlist from a radio station you discovered.
</Tip>

## Adding Songs to Playlists

### Add Individual Songs

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the Song">
    Browse or search for the song you want to add.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open Song Menu">
    Tap the three-dot menu icon next to the song.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Add to Playlist">
    Choose "Add to Playlist" from the menu.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Playlist">
    Select the playlist you want to add the song to, or create a new playlist.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Add from Now Playing

Add the currently playing song:

1. Open the Now Playing screen
2. Tap the three-dot menu
3. Select "Add to Playlist"
4. Choose your destination playlist

### Add Multiple Songs

Bulk add songs to playlists:

1. Navigate to an album, artist view, or search results
2. Tap "Select" or long-press a song to enter selection mode
3. Select multiple songs
4. Tap "Add to Playlist" from the action bar
5. Choose your destination playlist

## Editing Playlists

### Rename a Playlist

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Playlist">
    Navigate to the playlist you want to rename.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open Playlist Menu">
    Tap the three-dot menu at the top of the playlist.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Edit">
    Choose "Edit Playlist Details".
  </Step>

  <Step title="Update Name">
    Enter the new name and tap "Save".
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Change Playlist Artwork

1. Open the playlist
2. Tap the three-dot menu
3. Select "Edit Playlist Details"
4. Tap the current artwork
5. Choose a new image from your gallery or take a photo
6. Crop and adjust as needed
7. Save your changes

### Add Description

Add context to your playlists:

1. Open the playlist
2. Tap the three-dot menu > "Edit Playlist Details"
3. Enter a description in the description field
4. Save your changes

<Note>
  Descriptions help you remember the theme or purpose of a playlist, especially useful for mood-based or occasion-specific collections.
</Note>

## Organizing Playlist Content

### Reorder Songs

Arrange songs in your preferred order:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Playlist">
    Navigate to the playlist you want to reorder.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enable Edit Mode">
    Tap the edit icon or three-dot menu > "Reorder Songs".
  </Step>

  <Step title="Drag to Reorder">
    Press and hold the drag handle next to a song, then move it to your desired position.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Tap "Done" to save the new order.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Remove Songs from Playlist

Remove individual songs:

1. Open the playlist
2. Tap the three-dot menu next to the song you want to remove
3. Select "Remove from Playlist"

### Remove Multiple Songs

1. Open the playlist
2. Tap "Select" to enter selection mode
3. Select the songs you want to remove
4. Tap the delete icon
5. Confirm removal

### Sort Playlist

Automatically sort songs by different criteria:

1. Open the playlist
2. Tap the sort icon or three-dot menu > "Sort"
3. Choose sorting criteria:
   - Title (A-Z or Z-A)
   - Artist (A-Z or Z-A)
   - Date Added (Newest or Oldest first)
   - Duration (Shortest or Longest first)
   - Custom (manual order)

## YouTube Music Playlists

Musika syncs with your YouTube Music account to access your YouTube playlists.

### Sync YouTube Music Playlists

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign In">
    Make sure you're signed in to your YouTube Music account in Musika.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access Library">
    Navigate to Library > Playlists.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Refresh">
    Pull down to refresh and sync your YouTube Music playlists.
  </Step>

  <Step title="View Playlists">
    Your YouTube Music playlists will appear alongside local playlists.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Create YouTube Music Playlist

Create playlists directly on YouTube Music:

1. Navigate to Library > Playlists
2. Tap "+" > "Create YouTube Music Playlist"
3. Name your playlist
4. The playlist will be created on your YouTube Music account and synced

<Note>
  YouTube Music playlists created in Musika will be visible in the official YouTube Music app and website.
</Note>

### Edit YouTube Music Playlists

Modify your YouTube Music playlists:

- Add songs: Same process as local playlists
- Remove songs: Tap three-dot menu > "Remove from Playlist"
- Reorder: Use the drag handle in edit mode
- Changes sync automatically with YouTube Music

### Access Liked Songs

Your YouTube Music liked songs are available:

1. Go to Library
2. Find the "Liked Songs" playlist
3. This playlist automatically syncs with your YouTube Music likes

## Sharing Playlists

### Share Local Playlists

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Playlist">
    Navigate to the playlist you want to share.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Tap Share">
    Tap the share icon or three-dot menu > "Share".
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Export Format">
    Select how to share:
    - Export as file (M3U, JSON)
    - Share link (if synced with YouTube Music)
    - Share to social media
  </Step>

  <Step title="Send">
    Choose your sharing method and send to the recipient.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Import Playlists

Import playlists from other sources:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Import">
    Go to Library > Playlists > Menu > "Import Playlist".
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Source">
    Select the import method:
    - Import from file (M3U, JSON)
    - Import from YouTube Music link
    - Import from clipboard
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select File">
    Browse and select the playlist file to import.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm Import">
    Review the playlist contents and tap "Import".
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Smart Playlists

Create dynamic playlists that update automatically based on criteria:

1. Go to Library > Playlists
2. Tap "+" > "Create Smart Playlist"
3. Set criteria:
   - Genre
   - Artist
   - Recently played
   - Most played
   - Date added
4. Save the smart playlist

<Tip>
  Smart playlists are perfect for creating "Recently Added", "Most Played This Month", or genre-specific collections that update automatically.
</Tip>

## Collaborative Playlists

Create playlists that others can contribute to:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create or Open Playlist">
    Create a new playlist or open an existing one.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enable Collaboration">
    Tap three-dot menu > "Collaborative Playlist" > Enable.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Share Invite Link">
    Share the generated link with people you want to collaborate with.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Manage Collaborators">
    View and manage collaborators from the playlist settings.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Deleting Playlists

### Delete Local Playlist

<Warning>
  Deleting a playlist is permanent and cannot be undone. The songs themselves will remain in your library.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Playlist">
    Navigate to the playlist you want to delete.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open Menu">
    Tap the three-dot menu at the top of the playlist.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Delete">
    Choose "Delete Playlist".
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm">
    Confirm that you want to permanently delete the playlist.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Delete YouTube Music Playlist

1. Open the YouTube Music playlist
2. Tap three-dot menu > "Delete Playlist"
3. Confirm deletion

<Note>
  Deleting a YouTube Music playlist in Musika will also delete it from your YouTube Music account.
</Note>

## Playlist Tips & Best Practices

### Organization Strategies

- **By Mood**: Create playlists for different moods (Energetic, Relaxing, Focus)
- **By Activity**: Workout, Commute, Study, Party
- **By Time Period**: Morning Routine, Evening Wind-Down
- **By Genre**: Keep genres separate for easy browsing
- **By Occasion**: Road Trips, Dinner Party, Rainy Days

### Maintaining Playlists

1. **Regular Reviews**: Periodically review and remove songs you skip
2. **Keep Fresh**: Add new discoveries to keep playlists interesting
3. **Optimal Length**: 20-50 songs is ideal for most playlists
4. **Backup Important Playlists**: Export and save copies of playlists you've spent time curating

### Performance Tips

- Very large playlists (1000+ songs) may load slower
- Consider splitting large playlists into themed parts
- Download frequently played playlists for better performance
- Use smart playlists to automatically manage large collections
